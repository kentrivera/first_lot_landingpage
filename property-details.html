Property Description

Discover this exceptional 1-hectare parcel in the prestigious Sunset Valley, Bataan, where luxury living meets natural beauty. This prime piece of real estate offers the perfect canvas for your dream home, featuring breathtaking panoramic views of the Bataan mountains and Manila Bay.

The property boasts rare Maribeles River frontage with private access, providing endless opportunities for recreation and relaxation. Mature mango and narra trees provide natural privacy and shade, while the gently sloping terrain offers multiple ideal building sites with stunning vistas of the West Philippine Sea.

Located in an exclusive area known for its natural beauty and tranquility, yet just 30 minutes from Balanga City amenities, shopping, and dining. The property comes with all utilities available at the property line, including Meralco power and NAWASA water, making development straightforward and cost-effective.

This is truly a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to own a piece of paradise in one of the most sought-after locations in Bataan Province. Whether you're planning to build your forever home or looking for a premium investment opportunity, this property delivers on all fronts with easy access to Metro Manila via SCTEX.
